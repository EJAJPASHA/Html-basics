About section

Although the concept of computer based communities dated back to the early days of computer networks, only some years after the advent of internet, social networking have met public and commercial success. Computer network was initially envisioned in the heydays of The Beatles as a military-centric command and control scheme. But as it expanded beyond just a privileged few hubs and nodes, so too did the idea that connected computers might also make a great forum for discussing mutual topics of interest, and perhaps, even meeting or renewing acquaintance with other humans.
